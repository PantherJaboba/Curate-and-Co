## üì± IMMEDIATE NEXT STEPS

### **1. Connect Contact Form (5 minutes)**

**Use Formspree (Free):**
1. Go to **formspree.io**
2. Sign up (free)
3. Create a new form
4. Copy your form URL
5. Open `contact.html` in text editor
6. Find `<form id="contactForm">` (around line 50)
7. Change it to: `<form action="YOUR-FORMSPREE-URL" method="POST">`
8. Re-upload to Netlify (drag & drop again)

**OR Use Netlify Forms (if using Netlify):**
1. Open `contact.html`
2. Find `<form id="contactForm">`
3. Change to: `<form name="contact" netlify>`
4. Re-upload
5. Done! Netlify handles emails automatically.
