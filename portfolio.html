Meadow & Mallow Café

A cozy neighborhood café needed a digital presence as warm and inviting as their physical space. We created a content strategy that captured their homemade pastries, artisan coffee, and community-focused atmosphere.

Challenge

Meadow & Mallow had incredible foot traffic but struggled to translate their in-person charm to social media. They needed consistent, beautiful content that would attract new customers and keep regulars engaged.

Solution

We developed a warm, cottage-core aesthetic featuring natural lighting, close-up food photography, and behind-the-scenes moments. Our content calendar balanced product showcases with community stories and seasonal specials.

Results

  • 185% increase in Instagram followers in 6 months
  • 40% boost in weekend foot traffic attributed to social posts
  • Consistent 8-12% engagement rate
  • Featured in local food blog and magazine

A natural wellness brand needed a complete digital identity that conveyed their commitment to botanical healing, sustainability, and holistic health. We created a brand from the ground up.

Challenge

Impact

  • Sold out first product run within 3 weeks

The Thistle Room Home Decor

An established boutique home decor shop wanted to modernize their digital presence and reach a broader audience while maintaining their signature cottage-core aesthetic and artisan reputation.

Challenge

The Thistle Room had a loyal local customer base but minimal online presence. They needed to translate their curated in-store experience to digital platforms and build an e-commerce strategy without losing their boutique charm.

Solution

We created a cohesive digital ecosystem: refined social media presence showcasing products in styled settings, an updated website with e-commerce functionality, and email marketing to nurture their community. Content focused on home styling tips, artisan spotlights, and seasonal collections.

Transformation

  • 450% increase in online sales within 8 months
  • Expanded customer base to 12 new states
  • 75% email open rate (industry avg: 21%)
  • 12,000+ engaged Instagram followers
  • Successfully launched subscription box program
