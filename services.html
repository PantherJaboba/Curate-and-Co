Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require long-term contracts?

No! All packages are month-to-month. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you into contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What if I need to pause service?

Life happens! You can pause service for up to 2 months with a small holding fee, or cancel completely. We're flexible and understanding.

How quickly can you start?

Most clients start within 1-2 weeks after our discovery call. We'll work together on a content calendar and kick off your first posts as soon as you're ready.

Do you provide the images/photos?

It depends on your package. Starter clients provide photos (we'll give you shot lists!). Growth clients can add photography sessions. Pro clients get monthly professional photography included.

Can I switch packages later?

Absolutely! Many clients start with Starter and upgrade to Growth as they see results. You can scale up or down anytime based on your needs and budget.

What makes you different from agencies?

You work directly with us‚Äîno account managers or layers of bureaucracy. We get to know your business personally, create custom content (no templates), and genuinely care about your success. Plus, we're way more affordable!
