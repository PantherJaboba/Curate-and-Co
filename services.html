Common Questions

What if I need to pause or cancel?

No problem! All packages are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Just give us 30 days' notice if you need to pause or cancel.

Do you work with businesses outside Asheville/Hendersonville?

While we're based in the Asheville/Hendersonville area and love working with local businesses, we're happy to work with boutique brands anywhere who align with our values and aesthetic.

What platforms do you post to?

We focus primarily on Instagram and TikTok, as these platforms offer the best engagement for boutique brands. If you need Facebook posting, we can add that for an additional fee.

How do you create content?

We use a mix of your provided photos, stock imagery that matches your aesthetic, and custom graphics. Premium clients also get monthly professional photography sessions.

Can I upgrade or downgrade packages?

Absolutely! As your business grows (or if you need to scale back), you can switch packages at the start of any month. We're here to support you wherever you are.

What's the onboarding process like?

After booking your discovery call, we'll send a detailed questionnaire about your brand, schedule a strategy session, and typically launch your first content within 2 weeks.
